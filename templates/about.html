Contamination Risk Levels

Our system categorizes water samples into 4 distinct contamination levels based on the number of microplastics detected:

Clean (0 particles)

No microplastics detected. The water sample represents a safe and uncontaminated environment.

Low Risk (1-9 particles)

Minimal microplastics detected. This level indicates trace contamination, posing low environmental risk.

Moderate Risk (10-49 particles)

Noticeable microplastics detected. The water source shows signs of pollution and may pose moderate ecological risks.

Polluted (50+ particles)

Severe microplastics detected. This indicates significant contamination and high environmental concern.
